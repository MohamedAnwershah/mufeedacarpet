About Us

Our journey began with a clear vision — to deliver beautiful, long-lasting carpets at honest prices. Over the 15 years, our passion and experience have transformed what was once a small trading firm into a trusted name in the world of flooring. Today, we proudly offer a diverse range of premium styles, textures, and designs that bring comfort, elegance, and quality to every space we touch.

Vinyl Flooring for Workspaces

Our vinyl flooring is designed to enhance modern workspaces by combining professional style with long-lasting performance. With a stylish, sleek appearance, low maintenance, and high durability, it withstands daily traffic while keeping the floor looking fresh and professional for years.

2/2 Carpet for Modern Workspaces

Our 2/2 Carpet delivers a clean, modern look ideal for IT companies and start-up offices. Its strong woven texture handles heavy foot traffic, rolling chairs, and daily activity while keeping the space quiet and comfortable.

Sports & Outdoor Grass Mats

Designed for versatility and performance, our Grass Mats provide a durable, cushioned, and visually distinct solution for play areas, sports fields and outdoor spaces.
